Unit guidelines

Low risk guidelines are the same for men & women. both are advised not to drink more than 14 units a week.

What it looks like

6 pints of 4%beer

6 glasses of 13%wine

14 glasses of 30%spirits

How often

if you regularly drink 14 units per week, it's best to spread your drinking over 3 or more days. It's good to have several drink-free days a week.
