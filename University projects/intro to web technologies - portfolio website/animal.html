Context

Animal, often referred to as Wildlife, is a genre of Photography that is concerned with documenting the various forms of animals in their habitats. Our selected shows the inclusion of domestic animals, such as pets or farm which may typically be found in urban areas, or within the countryside.

Animal Photography requires specific Photography skills for the ability to capture moving, or unaware animals, whilst still maintaining good composure and composition. This often requires specific patience for the 'right moment' and may also need field craft skills. Many Photographers specialise specifically with Animal / Wildlife Photography due to its complexity and possibility to capture truly prodigious moments; often a risk / reward traid-off.
