Context

Candid Portraiture is a sub-topic of Street Photography. The aim of a Candid Photograph is to capture without creating a posed appearance. Our selected shows the inclusion of Street Portraiture, as well as capturing the subject in motion; i.e. they are doing something.

Candid Portraiture is the idea of avoiding posed appearances. This might be achieved such as; capturing the subject in motion, not distracting the subject during the photo being taken or avoiding any properation for the Photograph. It is this where Candid Portraiture requires specific skills; i.e. being able to frame a portait without the subjects intention or posing, whilst still maintaining correct composure and composition.
