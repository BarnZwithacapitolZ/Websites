Context

Still Life is a genre of Photography which is used to depict inanimate subject matter, such as a small group of objects. Still Life Photography gets its name from the application of the Still Life artistic style. Our selection shows a variety of Still Life forms, from food Photography to that of random inanimate object.

Still Life Photography is different from most other genres of Photography in that it allows the Photographer to fully express their artistic style. Often, the scene being photographed was created by the Photographer themself, so they can fully express their work by creating the scene's composition as they see fit. This is different from other genres of Photography, where the photographer is simply showing something, someone or somewhere; Still Life is more artistic and creative.
