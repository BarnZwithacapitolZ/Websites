Context

Street, often referred to as Candid is a type of Photography focused on capturing unmediated chance encounters and random incidents in public places. Our selection shows how Street Photography can be used in a more broader sense, representing the visualistic styles that can be portrayed through Street Photography, such as the typical objects which you typically pay no attention to on the street and their artistic value.

Street Photography typically is especially present for allowing the Photographer to express their artistic style through their Photographs; what is being framed? What is the composition of the image? What are we looking at? It is here where Street Photography differes from other genres, in allowing the Photographer more freedom of expression.
